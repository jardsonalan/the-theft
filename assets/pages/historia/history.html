History

A trio of old friends reunites after a long time with a single purpose: to make money.

Anastasya is a former Russian spy planning a major heist on a vault in the heart of the American capital. To pull it off, she counts on her old friends: Jack "Little Head" and Oliver "The Show-off." Knowing their dire situations, she was certain they would accept.

After years working for the United States Secret Police, Jack, an experienced hacker, found himself drowning in loan shark debts and lost his job. To avoid meeting a tragic end at the hands of the American mafia, he accepts the challenge.

Living a dull life with a regular job in the remote northern Canada, Oliver longs to relive his past filled with action and excitement as a soldier. Coincidentally, a message from an old friend arrives in his mailbox. The sender's name is simply signed: "-A".
